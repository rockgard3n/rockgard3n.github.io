Citi Personal Loan Payments

Served as the Product Owner for Personal Loan Payments. Lead the product from concept-to-launch to enable hundreds of thousands of customers to seamless pay tens of millions of dollars worth of personal loans. Payment products are by their nature cross-functional as well as financially, technically and legally complex. Therefore, in order to ship a high-quality, safe and secure user experience I collaborated with other Product teams as well as a variety of other external including: Design, UX Research, Development, Legal, and Retail. Additionally, I reduced payment processing time from 6 days to 1 day, drastically cut Citi’s costs by eliminating a third-party system, and helped increase personal loan enrollment by 30%.
